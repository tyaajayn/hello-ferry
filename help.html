FAQ

How to pay the tickets?                                 

Go to the ticket counter at the ferry terminal of the city you are departing from and pay the ticket upon redeeming.

What to bring to the ticket counter to redeem the ticket?                                 

Malaysian IC or Passport is a must along with the booking confirmation email.

How to cancel booking?

Fill in the form below this section. Give your name, booking number, cancellation reason. 

Can the departing city be changed?

No, you cannot. To do so, you must first cancel your current booking and make a new booking.
